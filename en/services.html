Google Ads

Built around real intent.

Search Ads

Show your business on Google when people search in a specific location using relevant keywords. Traffic is directed to the most relevant pages of your website to generate leads or inquiries.

The goal is not to push ads everywhere - it’s to show up when intent already exists.

  • Structured keyword segmentation by intent
  • Geo-targeted visibility control
  • Match type structure and negative keyword safeguard
  • Manual campaign structure (no blind automation)

Product catalog (PMAX/feed)

For inventory-based businesses, we connect your products so they appear across Google. Campaigns drive buyers directly to your listing pages.

This setup can run on its own, or be combined with Search campaigns when more control or scale is needed.

  • Clean product feed structure and normalization
  • Attribute mapping aligned with Google requirements
  • Feed-based segmentation for controlled distribution
  • Asset structure designed to support the feed (not replace it)

Reporting and supervision

As an option, you can add a monthly analysis with targeted adjustments and a clear report to help you understand the market you’re competing in.

This is not day-to-day management - it’s to verify campaigns are running properly and address clear issues over time.

  • Search term analysis and waste identification
  • Query isolation and intent refinement
  • Visibility and coverage monitoring
  • Period vs period reporting (Traffic, Conversions, Cost)
