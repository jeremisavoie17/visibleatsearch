Visibility aligned with demand

When someone searches on Google or Bing, they already have a specific need. They are not browsing. They are comparing. They are deciding.

Our work is not about pushing ads everywhere. It’s about showing up at the moment the decision is being made.

That’s why we focus exclusively on search-based visibility, where demand already exists.

  • Appear when people actively search for your services
  • Focus on demand, not unnecessary visibility
  • Avoid spending on low-intent exposure
  • Keep control over budget and scope

The foundation of the service - Google Search Ads

Your visibility is built using targeted Google Search Ads campaigns. This is the foundation of the service and what the initial setup covers.

Campaigns are structured to protect your brand name, clearly represent your main services, and communicate what makes your business different.

Google Ads is not used here to push advertising, but to appear when the intent already exists.

  • Protection of your business name
  • Targeting of your main service categories (up to 3-4)
  • Keyword selection based on real searches
  • Clear and focused ad messaging
  • Essential settings (targeting, extensions, negative keywords)

For ecommerce and product-based businesses

If you sell products online, we push your catalog directly into Google - across Search, Maps, YouTube and more - to capture high-intent buyers and drive them straight to your site.

This setup can run on its own, or be combined with search campaigns when more control or scale is wanted.

  • Product visibility across Google surfaces
  • High-intent traffic driven directly to your site
  • Standalone setup or paired with search
  • Ideal for ecommerce and product-focused businesses
