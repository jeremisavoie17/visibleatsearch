Visibility that matches demand

When someone searches on Google or Bing, they already have a specific need. They are not browsing. They are comparing. They are deciding.

Our work is not about pushing ads everywhere. It’s about showing up when that decision is happening - based on real intent.

That’s why we focus on search-based visibility only - where demand already exists.

  • Appear when people actively search for your service
  • Focus on demand, not awareness
  • Avoid useless exposure
  • Keep control over budget and scope

The core setup - Google Search Ads

Your visibility is built using targeted Google Search Ads campaigns. This is the foundation of the service and what the initial setup covers.

We structure campaigns so that your brand name is protected, your main services are clearly represented, and your ads communicate what makes you different.

Google Ads is not used here to “push” advertising. It’s used to show up when the intent already exists.

  • Protection of your business name
  • Targeting of your main service categories (up to 3-4)
  • Keyword selection based on real searches
  • Clear, focused ad messaging
  • Essential settings (targeting, extensions, negatives)

For ecommerce and product-based businesses

If you sell products online, we also offer a product-based visibility setup using Google Merchant Center and product ads.

Your product feed is connected directly to Google, allowing people to see your items and go straight to your website. This solution can be used on its own or combined with search campaigns to maximize coverage.

  • Product visibility directly on Google
  • Complementary to search campaigns
  • Ideal for ecommerce and artisans
  • Coverage across Search, Maps, YouTube and more

Step 1 - Immediate confirmation

You receive a confirmation email that summarizes what you purchased, what is included, and next steps.

Step 2 - Startup form

The email includes a link to a short startup form you can complete at your own pace. We ask for your business name, services offered, target area, and priorities (calls, forms, emails).

Step 3 - Analysis and setup

Once the form is completed, we analyze your business and local market, structure campaigns based on real searches, and prepare messaging and settings. If clarification is needed, we contact you by email.

When campaigns are ready, you receive a confirmation email and visibility starts based on agreed parameters. A report can cover traffic, conversions, CTR, CPC, CPA, impression share and competitive context.
