Google Search Ads

Your visibility is built using targeted Google Search Ads campaigns.

Campaigns are structured to protect your brand name, clearly represent your main services, and reach the market you want.

The goal is not to push ads everywhere - it’s to show up when intent already exists.

Product catalog (Performance Max / Shopping)

For product-based businesses, we connect your catalog so your products can appear directly on Google - Search, Maps, YouTube and more.

Campaigns are configured to capture active buyers and send them straight to your product pages.

This setup can run on its own, or be combined with Search campaigns when more control or scale is needed.

Monthly analysis and supervision

As an option, you can add a monthly analysis with targeted adjustments and a clear report.

This is not day-to-day management. It is a professional monthly inspection of your Google Ads account, to ensure campaigns are running properly, address performance issues and avoid wasting budget. Everything is presented in a clear report, to help you understand the market you’re competing in.
