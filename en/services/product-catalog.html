What the setup covers

Catalog connection

Merchant Center, website and Google Ads connections are reviewed so approved products or inventory can be used properly in campaigns.

Campaign structure

Catalog campaigns are structured around inventory, product groups, audience signals, search themes and assets to support qualified traffic.

Traffic safeguards

Brand exclusions, negative keywords and campaign settings are used where possible to reduce wasted spend and keep traffic more relevant.
