Problems management helps prevent

Management is not just about checking results. It is about keeping campaigns clean, measurable and active.

Compliance & delivery issues

Destination issues, feed errors or disapproved ads and assets can quietly limit performance or block ad delivery.

  • Merchant Center disapprovals
  • product feed errors
  • policy violations
  • destination issues
  • limited ad delivery

Poor tracking

If conversions are not measured properly, campaign decisions can be based on incomplete or misleading data.

  • missing conversions
  • duplicate leads
  • GA4 issues
  • tag errors
  • unclear lead quality

Wasted budget

Campaigns can slowly move away from the searches, audiences and signals that actually matter to your business.

  • irrelevant search queries
  • high-cost clicks
  • budget waste
  • targeting drift
  • weak traffic control

What management covers

Account alerts

Account alerts are continuously monitored for policy-related issues, billing alerts, delivery problems, disapproved ads, feed problems and major Google Ads notifications.

Client requests

Budget changes and urgent fixes can be requested anytime by email. Non-urgent updates, offer changes and time-consuming requests are grouped into your next scheduled support period.

Scheduled support work

Deep performance analysis, traffic quality control, campaign adjustments, reporting and strategic recommendations are completed based on your support plan.
