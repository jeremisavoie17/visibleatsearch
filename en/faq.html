FAQ

Simple answers to common questions.

Do you need to schedule a meeting or a call?

No. Everything is handled in writing through a short onboarding form and email exchanges. This keeps the service simple and affordable.

What do we need from you to get started?

You’ll complete a short onboarding form covering your services, target locations, goals, and competitor names if relevant. That’s enough to structure the campaigns.

Can you ask questions before committing?

Yes. You can email us anytime. Please allow up to 24 business hours for a response.

Can you ask questions after getting started?

Yes. Questions are always welcome. The account is set up to run independently after launch, while campaign changes are consolidated and applied during the scheduled 30-day review with reporting.

Can we target specific cities or regions across Canada?

Yes. Campaigns can be targeted by city, region, and service area. Competitor-related searches can also be included as keywords when it makes strategic sense.

Do you need a website or landing page?

Yes. You need a page that matches the ads and lets people contact you. Traffic is directed to the most relevant existing page.

How is conversion tracking handled?

You install a single Google Tag Manager code on your website (or ask your platform support to do it). Once this is done, all tracking configuration happens on our side, inside Google tools. We do not modify your website.

Are there any restrictions on what can be advertised?

Some regulated categories require certification, additional verification, or may be restricted by Google policies. If your category has special requirements, this will be flagged early in the setup process.

Why do we use a fixed fee instead of a percentage?

A fixed fee keeps costs predictable and accessible. You pay for the structure and optional supervision, not for ad spend volume.

Is there a minimum advertising budget?

No. There is no required minimum budget. You decide when to increase, reduce, or stop.

Who pays Google Ads?

You pay Google Ads directly through your own billing profile.

Who owns the account and do you get admin access?

The account belongs to you, with full admin access on your side. We are added as a partner to manage the setup and optional supervision.

Do you own your data?

Yes. Your data belongs entirely to you. You remain in control, even if you stop the supervision option.

How long before you see results?

It depends on your industry, local competition, and existing demand. The goal is not fast volume, but to be present when real demand exists. No fixed timeline can be guaranteed.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. No contracts, no hidden commitments.

Is monthly monitoring mandatory?

No. Monthly monitoring and reporting are completely optional.

Can you cancel the service?

Yes. Monthly supervision can be cancelled at any time with a 30-day notice.

The initial setup fee is non-refundable once work has started, as it covers custom work performed for your account. The setup is typically completed within 7 business days of purchase.
