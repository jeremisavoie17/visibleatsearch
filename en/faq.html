FAQ

Simple answers to common questions.

Do I need to schedule a meeting or a call?

No. Everything is done in writing, through a short onboarding form and email exchanges. This keeps the service simple and affordable.

Can I ask questions before committing?

Yes. You can email us at any time. Please allow 24 business hours for a response.

Can I ask questions after getting started?

The account is fully set up and ready to run independently after launch. Open discussion is always available, while all account changes are consolidated and applied during the scheduled 30-day review with reporting.

Is there a minimum advertising budget?

No. There is no required minimum budget. You decide when to increase, reduce, or stop.

Why a fixed fee instead of a percentage?

A fixed fee keeps the service accessible and makes costs predictable. You pay for the structure and optional supervision.

Is monthly monitoring mandatory?

No. Monitoring is completely optional.

How long before I see results?

It depends on your industry, your local market, and existing demand. The goal is not fast volume, but to be present at the right moment, when real demand exists.

I’ve tried online advertising before. Why would this be different?

Because the approach is different. We don’t try to do everything or sell everything. We focus on a clear, local, and intentional presence.

Do I own my data?

Yes. Your data belongs entirely to you. You remain in control, even if you stop the supervision option.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. No contracts, no hidden commitments.

Can I cancel the service?

Yes. Monthly supervision can be cancelled at any time with a 30-day notice.

The initial setup fee is non-refundable once work has started, as it covers custom work performed for your account. The setup is typically completed within 7 business days of purchase.
