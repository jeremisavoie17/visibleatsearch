FAQ

Simple answers to common questions.

Do we need a call to get started?

No. Everything is handled in writing through a short onboarding form and email. This keeps the process simple and affordable.

What do you need from us?

A short onboarding form covering your services, target locations, goals, and (if relevant) competitors. That’s enough to build a clean campaign structure.

Can we ask questions before or after we start?

Yes. Email us anytime. Most questions are answered within 24 business hours. Ongoing changes are typically consolidated during the scheduled 30-day review if you choose monthly supervision.

Do we need a website or landing page?

Yes. You need a page that matches the ads and allows people to contact you. We send traffic to the most relevant existing page.

Can you target specific cities or regions in Canada?

Yes. Campaigns can be targeted by city, region, and service area. Competitor-related searches can also be included when it makes strategic sense.

How is tracking handled?

You install one Google Tag Manager code on your website (or your platform support does it). Once installed, tracking setup happens inside Google tools. We do not modify your website.

Do I need a product feed?

Yes. A product feed must already exist or be provided by your website platform or site manager. We validate, configure, and use the feed within the Google ecosystem. If you tell us your platform (Shopify, WooCommerce, Wix, or custom), we’ll guide what to request or enable.

Are there any restrictions on what can be advertised?

Some regulated categories require certification, additional verification, or may be restricted by Google policies. If your category has special requirements, it will be flagged early in the setup.

Is there a minimum ad budget?

No. There is no required minimum. You control the budget and can increase, reduce, or stop anytime.

Who pays Google Ads?

You pay Google Ads directly through your own billing profile.

Do we own the account and the data?

Yes. The account and data are yours. You keep full admin access. We are added as admin to complete the setup and, if purchased, provide monthly supervision.

How long does it take to see results?

It depends on your industry, local competition, and existing demand. We focus on being present when real intent exists. No fixed timeline can be guaranteed.

Is monitoring mandatory or long-term?

No. Monthly supervision is optional and billed month-to-month. It is not included automatically with a setup purchase. If you choose supervision, you can cancel anytime with 30 days’ notice by email.

Setup fees are non-refundable once work has started.
