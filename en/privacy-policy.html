Privacy Policy

How Visible at Search handles information submitted through this website.

Effective date: May 28, 2026

Visible at Search is a Canada-based Google Ads service. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you visit this website, contact us or purchase a service.

Information we collect

We may collect the information you choose to provide, including your name, email address, business name, website, service area, advertising needs and any message or onboarding details you submit.

Basic website usage data may also be collected through tools such as Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager and Google Ads. This can include pages viewed, traffic source, device type and general interaction data.

How we use information

Information is used to respond to requests, prepare or deliver services, review advertising needs, manage Google Ads work, provide support by email and improve the website.

Forms and payments

Some forms on this website may be powered by Tally. Information submitted through forms is used only to respond to requests, prepare services or complete onboarding.

Payments are processed securely through Square. Visible at Search does not store full payment card details on this website.

Google account access

Some services may require access to platforms such as Google Ads, Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager or Google Merchant Center.

Access is used only to complete the requested setup, review, tracking or management work. Clients keep ownership and control of their own accounts.

Sharing of information

Visible at Search does not sell personal information.

Information may be handled through the tools needed to operate the website and deliver services, including email, forms, analytics, payment processing and advertising platforms.

Data retention

Information is kept only as long as reasonably needed to provide services, maintain business records, respond to requests or meet administrative requirements.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect information. However, no website, email system or online platform can be guaranteed to be completely secure.
