Founder of Visible at Search

Visible at Search was founded and is led by Jérémi Savoie, a Google Ads specialist based in New Brunswick.

The service was created to offer a more direct, clearer and more accessible way to launch or improve Google Ads campaigns, without going through a heavy agency structure.

The goal is simple: help businesses gain stronger visibility in Google, with structured campaigns, clear conversion tracking and an approach focused on real results.

Hands-on experience focused on performance

Visible at Search is built on more than 5 years of digital marketing experience for an automotive dealer group, along with more than 10 years in retail-related environments involving web inventory, online sales, social media, merchandising and lead quality analysis.

Experience with larger Google Ads budgets directly shapes the model used today. What truly matters in competitive markets: a well-built campaign structure, targeted search intent, clear conversion measurement and disciplined execution.

That background helps build Google Ads campaigns aligned with real business priorities: local demand, lead quality, conversion tracking, available inventory, expansion potential and growth decisions.
