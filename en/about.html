About

Google Ads service based in New Brunswick, Canada

Founder of Visible at Search

Visible at Search was founded by Jérémi Savoie, a Google Ads specialist based in New Brunswick.

The goal is simple: to offer a more direct and more accessible way to launch a structured Google Ads account, without going through a heavy agency model.

The model is designed to stay aligned with your needs: setup is billed once and support can be added only when needed.

Hands-on experience focused on performance

Visible at Search is built on more than 5 years of digital marketing experience for an automotive dealer group, along with more than 10 years in retail-related environments involving web inventory, online sales, social media, merchandising and lead quality analysis.

Experience managing large annual Google Ads budgets across multi-location business environments shapes how we operate today. What truly matters in competitive markets: a well-built campaign structure, targeted search intent, clear conversion measurement and disciplined execution.

100% by email, direct access

Everything starts with a simple online form. We take the time to understand your business and where visibility truly matters. Setup is handled for you from start to finish.

Visible at Search is built around a deliberately lean model. Communication is handled by email so everything stays clear, documented and easy to follow. There are no sales representatives, no intermediary account managers and no unnecessary layers between you and the person actually working on your campaigns.

Based in New Brunswick, serving businesses here and beyond

Visible at Search is based in New Brunswick, with a practical understanding of local, rural and regional realities, as well as solid experience with urban, metropolitan and more competitive markets.

The service is fully online and built to support businesses across Canada through clear written communication and a structured process.
