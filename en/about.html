About

Google Ads service based in New Brunswick, Canada

Our approach

It starts with a simple online form. We take the time to understand your business, where visibility matters most, and what needs to be set up properly.

The model is designed to stay aligned with your needs, without forcing more service than necessary. Setup is billed once. You choose the budget based on your market potential.

Everything is handled for you from start to finish. Add support based on the level of monitoring, adjustments and reporting you want for your campaigns.

How we operate

We operate with a deliberately lean structure to maximize value for our customers. With no agency overhead or long-term dependency, the process stays simple, direct and efficient.

There are no sales consultants, no intermediary account managers, and no unnecessary layers. You work directly with the person responsible for strategy and execution.

The process starts online with a simple form, then communication continues by email so requests stay documented and easy to follow. You can move at your own pace, without unnecessary meetings or management overhead.

Why us

Visible at Search is founded and led by a direct Google Ads specialist based in New Brunswick, with more than 5 years of digital marketing experience for a large automotive group and more than 10 years in retail-related environments involving web inventory, online sales, social media and CRM/BDC follow-up.

Experience managing seven-figure annual Google Ads budgets shapes the model we use today. Working with large, complex accounts shows what actually matters in competitive markets: clear campaign structure, relevant search intent, clean tracking priorities and disciplined execution.

That background helps build campaigns that are not only clean inside Google Ads, but also aligned with how businesses actually operate: inventory, customer demand, lead quality, conversion tracking, sales follow-up and growth decisions.
