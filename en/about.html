About

Google Ads service based in New Brunswick, Canada

Founder of Visible at Search

Visible at Search was founded and is led by Jérémi Savoie, a Google Ads specialist based in New Brunswick.

The goal is simple: to offer a more direct and more accessible way to launch or improve a structured Google Ads account, without going through a heavy agency structure.

The model is designed to stay aligned with your needs: setup is billed once and support can be added only when needed.

Hands-on experience focused on performance

Visible at Search is built on more than 5 years of digital marketing experience for an automotive dealer group, along with more than 10 years in retail-related environments involving web inventory, online sales, social media, merchandising and lead quality analysis.

Experience with larger Google Ads budgets directly shapes the model used today. What truly matters in competitive markets: a well-built campaign structure, targeted search intent, clear conversion measurement and disciplined execution.

100% by email, direct access

Everything starts with a simple online form. We take the time to understand your business, where visibility truly matters and what needs to be configured properly. Setup is handled for you from start to finish.

Visible at Search offers a deliberately lean structure. Communication is handled by email so everything stays clear, documented and easy to follow. There are no sales representatives, no intermediary account managers and no unnecessary layers between you and the person actually working on your campaigns.

Based in New Brunswick, serving businesses here and beyond

Visible at Search is based in New Brunswick, with a practical understanding of local, rural and regional realities, as well as solid experience with urban, metropolitan and more competitive markets.

Google Ads campaigns can serve very different contexts: local services, competitive urban businesses or markets to develop elsewhere in Canada.
