While Google Analytics (GA4) can work alone for basic visits, Google Tag Manager (GTM) is essential for tracking precise actions such as call clicks, form submissions, and key button interactions without modifying your website code every time.

With our setup method, we use DebugView in Google Analytics (GA4) to validate events in real time. Official data usually starts appearing in standard reports within 24 to 48 hours.

We track form submissions through Google Tag Manager (GTM) using a reliable trigger such as a thank-you page, form event, or DOM-based trigger depending on your site. The event is then sent to Google Analytics (GA4) and marked as a conversion where relevant.

We track clicks on phone links and call buttons using Google Tag Manager (GTM) click triggers. The event is sent to Google Analytics (GA4) and can also be imported into Google Ads so campaigns optimize toward valuable actions.
