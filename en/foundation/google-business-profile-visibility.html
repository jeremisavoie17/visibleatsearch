What is Google Business Profile

Google Business Profile is the platform used to manage how a business appears on Google Search and Google Maps. It helps businesses control visible details such as name, address, phone number, hours, categories, reviews, and directions.

Business information

Management of core details such as name, address, phone number, hours, and categories to keep listings accurate and consistent.

Google Maps visibility

Visibility in Google Maps and local search results when people search by location.

Location assets

Connection of business location information with Google Ads so campaigns can support local visibility.

What drives local visibility

Google Business Profile visibility is influenced by several signals. Accurate business information and integration with Google Ads location assets help reinforce local presence.

Business name and category

Clear business name and relevant categories help Google understand what the business is and when it should appear in results.

Accurate information

Consistent details such as address, phone number, and opening hours help maintain trust and support local search visibility.

Google Ads location assets

Integration with Google Ads location assets connects business location data with campaigns and supports stronger visibility.
