What is a Google Ads account review

A Google Ads account review is a structured analysis of how campaigns are built, measured and optimized. The goal is to understand how the account captures search demand, how budgets are used and how conversion tracking supports decisions.

Account health check

Review of campaign structure, ad groups, keyword targeting and exclusions.

Intent and cost analysis

Evaluation of search intent alignment, CPC patterns and budget distribution.

Market signals

Analysis of competitor presence, auction insights and visibility patterns.

Which areas are analyzed

A useful review connects campaign structure, search intent, measurement quality and traffic signals. The objective is not just to look at clicks, but to understand whether the account is built to generate qualified leads efficiently.

Campaign structure

Campaign organization, ad group segmentation and keyword coverage.

Search intent

Alignment between search queries, keywords and ad targeting.

Conversion tracking

Google Ads conversions, GA4 integration and measurement reliability.

Traffic channels

Paid Search, Cross-network, Organic and channel attribution signals.

How the review works
